Group 02 — Drainage Canal Flow Analysis

Compute Total
Water Volume
Numerically.

Upload velocity samples from a drainage canal, choose your CSV mapping, pick the section area & sampling step. The console runs Rectangle, Trapezoidal, and Simpson's rules to estimate V = A · ∫v(t) dt and renders an oscilloscope-style telemetry feed.

Quick Reference

  • Section area A — constant (m²)
  • Sampling step h — seconds between v readings
  • Rectangle = left-endpoint sum
  • Trapezoidal = linear interpolation
  • Simpson = quadratic, highest accuracy
